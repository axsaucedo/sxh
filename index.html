/ SXHacks

The world's first Hackathon in the

London Stock Exchange.

HackTrain is the first and biggest initiative in Europe targeted to #HackTheRails! An unforgettable journey where a cohort of 40 of the best developers will jump on a once-in-a-lifetime journey, competing in the HackTrain hackathon, and obtaining a chance to join the HackTrain accelerator program! You will jump aboard the HackTrain for an opportunity to travel to the top coworking spaces in the UK, hang out with the market makers in the Startup ecosystem, and create startups that will revolutionize the transport and travel industries!

Startups, apps, prototypes will be created, and presented for funding to of CEOs, investors, VCs, serial entrepreneurs and evangelists - all with the objective of becoming the 2015 HackTrain champion! With TrainHackers from UK, Russia, Romania, France, Poland, Czech Republic together with many other European countries, this hackathon will certainly promise a unique experience!

Crazy? Definitely! Impossible? Not even close! HackTrain alumni have created products that have been taken forward after prototyping mind blowing and revolutionary concepts! Some of our past events include our 2015 HackTrain, our Startup Bus UK 2014, Startup Weekend Moscow, Startup Hacks.

Thanks to our strong partnership with the largest train companies in the UK, including East Midland Trains and Virgin Trains East Coast, hackers will be turning pizza into software whilst on an executive First Class Journey!
